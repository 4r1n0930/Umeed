About SAI

The Sports Authority of India (SAI) is the apex national sports body of India, established in 1984 by the Ministry of Youth Affairs and Sports. SAI manages various sports disciplines and training programs across the country.

Our mission is to promote sports and games in the country, identify and nurture young talent, and provide them with the necessary infrastructure and coaching to excel at national and international levels.

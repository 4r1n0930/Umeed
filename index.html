Ballers Are Awesome!

Satnam Singh Bhamara

First Indian-born basketball player to be drafted into the NBA. At 7'2", this towering center made history when selected by the Dallas Mavericks in 2015, paving the way for Indian talent on the global stage.

Amjyot Singh

Versatile forward known for his impressive international performances. Represented India in multiple FIBA Asia Cups and became the first Indian to play in the Japanese B.League, showcasing exceptional skill and athleticism.

Pratima Singh

Trailblazing point guard and captain of the Indian women's basketball team. Led India to multiple international victories with her exceptional ball-handling skills and leadership on court.

Vishesh Bhriguvanshi

Dynamic shooting guard and captain of the Indian men's team. Known for his scoring prowess and clutch performances, he has been a consistent force in Indian basketball for over a decade.

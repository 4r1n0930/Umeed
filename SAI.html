SAI Schemes & Disciplines

SAI Schemes

Khelo India Scheme

A national program for the development of sports, aiming at mainstreaming sports as a tool for individual development and national pride.

National Sports Talent Contest (NSTC)

Identifies sporting talent among students in the age group of 8-12 years and nurtures them by providing scientific training.

Army Boys Sports Companies (ABSC)

Provides training to talented sports persons in the age group of 8-16 years in selected disciplines at various Army centers.

Special Area Games (SAG)

Scouts and nurtures talent from tribal, rural, and coastal areas of the country in specific sports disciplines.

Centre of Excellence (COE)

Provides world-class training facilities and coaching to elite athletes to help them excel at international competitions.

Women Sports Promotion Scheme

Encourages participation of women in sports and provides special training and facilities for female athletes.
